Toast!

Perfect for breakfast or anytime, there's nothing quite like a delicious slice of toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of fresh sourdough bread
  • Softened butter
  • Strawberry or Blackbery Jam

Directions

  1. Toast for approximately 2 minutes. Watch carefully that it doesn't burn.
  2. Quickly spread the butter across the entire surface. Don't be skimpy!
  3. Spread the jam over the butter. It doesn't take as much as you'd think.
  4. Cut diagonally into 2 triangles. Eat hot for best results.
  5. Optional: try puzzle toast if preparing for someone else!
